The problem

Traditional search fails when knowledge is complex, distributed, and interconnected. Your answers are buried in there — finding them takes hours.

Searching "maintenance procedure" returns 847 results. None answer: "Which procedures apply to Class III equipment inspected over 18 months ago?" You need intelligence, not index matching.

02
